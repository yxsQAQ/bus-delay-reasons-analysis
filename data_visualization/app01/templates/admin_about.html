Definition of Bus Delay Severity

In order to quantify the degree of bus delay in a systematic manner, the study employs a specific mathematical formula designed to calculate the resultant delay metrics.
Degree of delay = absolute value(Expected bus arrival time - real bus arrival time) / (destination time - departure time)
